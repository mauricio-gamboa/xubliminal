Bringing some sweetness

The Challenge

It all started with a request for a face lift of their web sites homepage and eventual updates on some other important pages to increase their conversion rate for first-time visitors. We were very excited to work on this project from start to finish and the design was a bit outdated, boring and confusing.

our solution

Once we delivered a more refreshing proposal for their home page we worked closely with them and provided better versions that were hard to choose from. Along the way there were a bunch of new design elements to do a whole new concept to redesign.

The result

The folks at Xubliminal are really special. Unlike others, they quickly understood what we needed and presented us with a variety of suitable designs. After we had already given the final OK for the design, they came up with an improved version that they (and we) liked even better. That's what I call pro-active. The communication during the design and implementation phases was really straight-forward and they were no problems that could not be solved. We're very happy with the result and we're looking forward to working with them on future projects.
